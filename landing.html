BDH is an organization committed to providing the best care, community involvement, and quality of life for the MHMR and the special needs community. BDH is a large family of consumers who love being there as much as we love having them.


We believe that everyone wants to be understood, heard, and loved. We also believe that in order to gain respect, we must give it.


Each consumer has a separate set of needs. We are still a small enough workshop to be able to meet each set of needs. When a consumer has a passion or a goal, we like to focus on that. We also provide job placement assistance and supported employment. We have a full functioning computer lab, arts/crafts room, and gaming/movie room.
