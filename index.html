AMAZING EXPERIENCE

BDH is an organization committed to providing the best care, community involvement, and quality of life for the special needs community. BDH is a family of consumers who love being there as much as we love having them.

Wanting to be understood, heard, and treaded with care is what we believe in. To gain respect from others we believe you must give it first.


Every single one of the consumers we have has a set of needs. Our workplace is small enough to reach everyone's set of needs. When a consumer has a passion or a goal, we like to focus our full attention on that. We also provide job placement assistance and supported employment. We have a full computers, arts/crafts, and games/movies.
