WHAT WE DO

Bonistar is a self-sustaining, master-planned, integrated community where adults, with intellectaul and developmental disabilites can learn and grow emotionally.

They will also interact socially with their nondiabled peers with the goal of promoting independence and opportunities.

The men and women in our program participate in a variety of activites. Bonistar's life learning center educates and trains adults on how to be independent.



Programs

We offer a variety of Social, Physical, and Educational Activities

  • Games
  • Sports
  • Arts
  • Computer Skills
  • Outing

Transportation

We offer transportation to our facilities, for groups or individual consumers without access to transportation, at a reasonable rate.
